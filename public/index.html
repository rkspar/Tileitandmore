History

In 1986 the company was founded by Steven Silverman out of the back of his truck as a tile installer.

In 1989 Tile It was incorporated at Tile It, Inc.

In 2002 he moved the business into a small 1000 sft warehouse in Manalapan, NJ.

In 2005 he moved into our former 2,000 sft warehouse in Manalapan, NJ.

In 2010 we moved into our current 9,000 sft warehouse in Manalapan, NJ, and changed our name to Tile It and More.
